My Fourth Year Design Project

Every engineering student at the University of Waterloo must complete a fourth year design/capstone project to show off our creative ideas. My group focused on designing an atmospheric water generator that condenses moisture from humid air to create potable, drinking water.

Ryan Jung, 2016. Contact: r2jung@uwaterloo.ca
