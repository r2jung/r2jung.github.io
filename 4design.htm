Our Design

Our unit utilizes a Peltier "Thermoelectric" effect to generate water through atmospheric condensation. This produces a temperature gradient, where the heat is rejected to the environment by way of an air multiplier.


How it Works

The Peltier unit itself operates on 12V/5A DC power, and is capable of generating temperatures near 0 C on its cold surface.
